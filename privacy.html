Overview

Isolyr Labs collects only the data needed to operate, maintain, and secure its systems. This page is the primary policy and links to concise companion documents for specific topics.

Our goals are clarity, minimal data collection, and controlled use. We avoid legal clutter and state plainly what happens with your data.

Scope

  • Applies to Isolyr Labs services: Discord bots, Minecraft plugins, experimental systems, and contracted work.
  • Covers data we collect, use, store, or share while delivering those services.
  • Complementary pages expand on each topic for transparency and quick reference.

Baseline Commitments

  • No data sales. No advertising use.
  • Only collect what is necessary for the service to function or to secure it.
  • Retain data only as long as needed for the stated purpose.
  • Use reputable platforms and apply reasonable safeguards; no system is perfectly secure.
