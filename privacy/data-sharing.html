Data Sharing

When data leaves our systems, with whom, and why.

Last updated: December 19, 2025

We Do Not

  • Do not sell or rent data.
  • Do not share data for advertising or marketing.

When We Share

  • With platform providers required for operation (e.g., Discord, Mojang) under their policies.
  • With infrastructure providers (cloud, hosting, error logging) to run and secure services.
  • When legally required, after verifying the request and scope.

Controls

  • Limit shared data to what the task requires.
  • Restrict access to authorized operators and automation.
  • Review integrations periodically to confirm necessity.

Incident Response

If sharing is needed to investigate or contain abuse or security issues, scope remains narrow and time-bound to the incident.
