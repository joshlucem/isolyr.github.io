Data Usage

How collected data is used and what is excluded.

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Allowed Uses

  • Deliver requested features (bot commands, plugin behaviors, contracted functions).
  • Maintain reliability and performance (monitoring, profiling within service scope).
  • Debug and mitigate incidents or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations when they apply.

Explicitly Not Used For

  • No advertising or marketing use.
  • No sale or rental of data.
  • No creation of behavioral or tracking profiles.
  • No automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

Minimization in Practice

  • Use the smallest necessary payload for each feature.
  • Prefer aggregated or ephemeral data for diagnostics.
  • Separate test data from production data and purge test data promptly.

Security and Abuse Response

Data may be used to detect, investigate, and respond to abuse, fraud, or security events affecting the service. Actions stay scoped to protecting the service and its users.
