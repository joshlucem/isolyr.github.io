When This Policy Does Not Apply

Scope boundaries, third parties, and separate agreements.

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Third-Party Platforms

  • Discord and Mojang handle account data under their own privacy policies.
  • We do not control how these platforms process or store their data.

Customer-Managed Environments

  • Servers or infrastructure you operate are governed by your own policies.
  • If you install our plugins or bots, ensure your users are informed under your terms.

Contract-Specific Terms

  • Custom engagements may define data handling separately; those terms take precedence for the contracted scope.
  • We still aim for minimal collection and clear retention even under bespoke terms.

External Links and Integrations

  • Links to third-party sites are outside this policy; review their terms.
  • User-configured integrations (webhooks, APIs) follow the destination service’s rules.
