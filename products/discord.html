Scriptor

An experience management system designed around writing.

Messages generate small amounts of XP, accumulated slowly and deliberately. There are no shortcuts, no spam rewards, and no instant progression.

Core Features

  • Writing-based XP system with controlled decimal precision
  • Six structured levels with role rewards
  • Ephemeral user messages to avoid channel clutter
  • Anti-exploit logic to prevent farming and abuse
  • Embed-based configuration using buttons, selects, and modals
  • Brown-toned interface with a restrained visual style

Philosophy

Scriptor is intentionally demanding. It does not collect unnecessary data and operates as a system, not a spectacle.
