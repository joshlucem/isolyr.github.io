AirwaveChat

Realistic radio communication for your Minecraft server

Features

Frequency System

  • AM Band: 1000–1999 kHz (≈100 block range)
  • FM Band: 100.0–199.9 MHz (≈40 block range)
  • Custom Channels: Create named frequencies with custom ranges
  • 1000+ Available Frequencies: Plenty of channels for your players

Realistic Radio Effects

  • Proximity Chat: Messages reach players within range only
  • Signal Degradation: Text distorts with static at distance
  • Live Signal Bar: Real-time strength indicator in the action bar
  • Radio Sounds: Authentic connect / disconnect / static audio

Player Experience

  • Persistent Connections: Auto-reconnect to your last frequency on login
  • Frequency Search: Find channels by name quickly
  • Top Channels: See most popular frequencies
  • Smart Pagination: Browse frequency lists with ease

Customization

  • MiniMessage Support: Rich text formatting with hex colors
  • Configurable Distances: Adjust range per band
  • Static Control: Fine-tune distortion threshold and intensity
  • Sound Options: Choose and toggle sound effects
  • Cooldown System: Prevent command spam

Use Cases

Roleplay Servers: Police, EMS, and civilian channels
Survival: Coordinate with your team across distances
PvP: Strategic team communication
Minigames: Team-based modes with radio coordination

Requirements

Paper/Spigot/Folia 1.20.4+ • Java 21+
